Here are my quick notes on classical mechanics.

First Law (Inertia): An object will not change its motion unless a force acts on it.

Second Law (F=ma): The force on an object is equal to its mass times its acceleration. (This is super important for the midterm!)
📐 [ Hand-drawn Force Vector Diagram ]
Third Law: When two objects interact, they apply forces to each other of equal magnitude and opposite direction.
